Historical Places of India


Taj Mahal


The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built between 1631 and 1648 by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan as a tomb for his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal


India Gate

India Gate is a 42-meter tall war memorial in New Delhi, India that honors the soldiers who died fighting for the British Army in World War I.


Red Fort

The Red Fort, also known as Lal Qila, is a historic fort complex in Delhi, India that was built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan between 1638 and 1648.


Ayodhya Ram

Ram Mandir or Ram Temple is a Hindu temple in Ayodhya, a city in Uttar Pradesh, India.According to Hindus, the site is the birthplace of the Hindu god Rama.
